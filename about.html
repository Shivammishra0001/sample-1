About PathCare Diagnostics

Your trusted partner in health diagnostics since 2010. Committed to accuracy, quality, and compassionate patient care.

Laboratory Facility Image

Years of Excellence

Dedicated to Your Health & Well-being

PathCare Diagnostics was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide accurate, affordable, and accessible diagnostic services to everyone.

Since our establishment in 2010, we have grown from a single laboratory to a trusted network of diagnostic centers serving thousands of patients across the region. Our state-of-the-art facilities, combined with our team of experienced pathologists and technicians, ensure that every test we conduct meets the highest standards of quality and accuracy.

We understand that diagnostic tests are often the first step in understanding your health. That's why we are committed to delivering not just results, but peace of mind through reliable diagnostics and compassionate care.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and affordable diagnostic services while maintaining the highest standards of quality and patient care.

Our Vision
