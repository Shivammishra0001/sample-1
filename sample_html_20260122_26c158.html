About MediLab Diagnostics

Home / About Us

Our Commitment to Accurate Diagnostics

Established in 2010, MediLab Diagnostics has been serving the community with reliable and precise diagnostic services. We believe that accurate test results are the foundation of effective healthcare.

Our mission is to provide high-quality diagnostic services with compassion, integrity, and technical excellence. We are committed to making healthcare more accessible through our affordable pricing and convenient services.

With a team of experienced pathologists and state-of-the-art technology, we ensure that every test is performed with the highest standards of accuracy and reliability.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and affordable diagnostic services that contribute to better health outcomes for our patients. We aim to be the most trusted diagnostic partner for individuals and healthcare providers.

Our Vision

To become a leading diagnostic center known for excellence, innovation, and patient-centric care. We envision a community where preventive healthcare through accurate diagnostics is accessible to all.

Blood Tests

Comprehensive range of blood tests for diagnosis and monitoring of health conditions

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Measures different components of blood including red cells, white cells, and platelets.

Lipid Profile

Measures cholesterol and triglyceride levels to assess cardiovascular risk.

Liver Function Test

Evaluates the health of your liver by measuring enzymes, proteins, and substances.

Kidney Function Test

Assesses how well your kidneys are working through various blood markers.

Thyroid Profile

Measures thyroid hormone levels to diagnose thyroid disorders.

Blood Sugar Tests

Fasting, postprandial, and HbA1c tests for diabetes diagnosis and management.
