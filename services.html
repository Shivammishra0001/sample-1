Tests & Services

Comprehensive diagnostic tests with accurate results. Browse our wide range of blood tests, screenings, and specialized diagnostics.

Blood Tests

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Measures red blood cells, white blood cells, hemoglobin, hematocrit, and platelets to assess overall health.

Results: 6-8 hours Fasting: Not required

Blood Glucose (Fasting)

Measures blood sugar levels after fasting. Essential for diabetes screening and monitoring.

Results: 4-6 hours Fasting: 8-12 hours

HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin)

Provides average blood sugar levels over the past 2-3 months for diabetes management.

Results: 24 hours Fasting: Not required

Lipid Profile

Measures cholesterol levels including total cholesterol, HDL, LDL, and triglycerides for heart health assessment.

Results: 24 hours Fasting: 10-12 hours

Thyroid Profile (T3, T4, TSH)

Comprehensive thyroid function test to diagnose thyroid disorders and monitor treatment.

Results: 24 hours Fasting: Not required

Vitamin D Test

Measures vitamin D levels in blood to detect deficiency which can affect bone health.

Results: 24-48 hours Fasting: Not required

Organ Function Tests

Liver Function Test (LFT)

Assesses liver health by measuring enzymes, proteins, and bilirubin levels in blood.

Results: 24 hours Fasting: 8-10 hours

Kidney Function Test (KFT)

Evaluates kidney health by measuring creatinine, urea, and other markers.

Results: 24 hours Fasting: Not required

Cardiac Markers

Tests including Troponin-I, CPK-MB to assess heart muscle damage and cardiac health.

Results: 6-8 hours Fasting: Not required

Pancreatic Function Test

Measures amylase and lipase levels to evaluate pancreatic health and function.

Results: 24 hours Fasting: 8 hours

Electrolyte Panel

Measures sodium, potassium, chloride, and bicarbonate levels for fluid balance assessment.

Results: 6-8 hours Fasting: Not required

Uric Acid Test

Measures uric acid levels to diagnose gout and monitor kidney function.

Results: 24 hours Fasting: Not required

Special Diagnostics

Allergy Panel

Comprehensive allergy testing to identify food and environmental allergens causing reactions.

Results: 3-5 days Fasting: Not required

Hormonal Profile

Tests for various hormones including cortisol, testosterone, estrogen, and progesterone.

Results: 24-48 hours Fasting: Varies

Vitamin B12 & Folate

Measures vitamin B12 and folic acid levels essential for nerve function and red blood cell production.

Results: 24-48 hours Fasting: Not required

Iron Studies

Complete iron panel including serum iron, TIBC, and ferritin to diagnose anemia and iron disorders.

Results: 24 hours Fasting: 8-12 hours

Tumor Markers

Screening tests including PSA, CA-125, CEA for early detection and monitoring of cancers.

Results: 24-48 hours Fasting: Not required

Infectious Disease Panel

Tests for HIV, Hepatitis B & C, and other infectious diseases with confidential reporting.

Results: 24-48 hours Fasting: Not required
